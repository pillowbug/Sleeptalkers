Hallelujah EP | 2020

Sleeptalkers is a one-two punch of nostalgia and energy blending infectious melodies and upbeat drive.

Sleeptalkers was started in the winter of 2015 by frontman Gerard Cabarse. After enlisting his long-time friend Ryan Astudillo to drums and bringing Kevin Wong and Ryan Wong (no relation) to round out the group, the group began playing their brand of pop rock across the San Francisco Bay Area. Drawing inspiration from personal experience, pop culture, and introspection, Sleeptalkers explores self-improvement, love, and staying positive, especially when it feels impossible to.
